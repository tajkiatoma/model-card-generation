## Quantitative Analyses
This section presents disaggregated evaluation results.

### Unitary results:
*   **Conversational Performance (vs. Open Models):** In a comparison over 6 languages, BLOOMChat achieved an average win rate of **65.92%** against BLOOMZ (1.46%), OpenAssistant-30B (23.16%), and LLaMA-Adapter-V2-65B (7.55%) (Source: `image-1.png`).
*   **Conversational Performance (vs. GPT-4):** In a head-to-head comparison over 6 languages, BLOOMChat achieved a win rate of **44.97%** against GPT-4's 55.03% (Source: `image-2.png`).
*   **Translation Performance:** BLOOMChat achieved an average BLEU score of approximately **20.2** across French, Hindi, and Chinese translation tasks. This score was higher than BLOOM, BLOOMZ, OpenAssistant-30B, and Vicuna-13B, but lower than GPT-4 (approximately 31.0) (Source: `image-3.png`).
