### Model type:
This is a `StableDiffusionPipeline` model, which is a text-to-image generation model (Source: `model_index.json.txt`). It consists of several key components:

*   **Model Architecture:** The pipeline is composed of a Variational Autoencoder (VAE), a U-Net model, a text encoder, a tokenizer, and a scheduler (Source: `model_index.json.txt`).
*   **Variational Autoencoder (VAE):** An `AutoencoderKL` model that encodes images into a latent space and decodes latent representations back into images.
    *   **Latent Channels:** 4 (Source: `vae/config.json.txt`).
    *   **Input/Output Channels:** 3 (Source: `vae/config.json.txt`).
    *   **Scaling Factor:** 0.18215 (Source: `vae/config.json.txt`).
    *   **Sample Size:** 256 (Source: `vae/config.json.txt`).
*   **U-Net:** A `UNet2DConditionModel` that operates in the latent space to denoise the latent image representation based on the text prompt.
    *   **Input Channels:** 4 (Source: `unet/config.json.txt`).
    *   **Output Channels:** 4 (Source: `unet/config.json.txt`).
    *   **Cross Attention Dimension:** 768 (Source: `unet/config.json.txt`).
    *   **Activation Function:** `silu` (Source: `unet/config.json.txt`).
*   **Text Encoder:** A `CLIPTextModel` based on `openai/clip-vit-large-patch14` which creates text embeddings from input prompts (Source: `model_index.json.txt`, `text_encoder/config.json.txt`).
    *   **Architecture:** 12 hidden layers, 12 attention heads, and a hidden size of 768 (Source: `text_encoder/config.json.txt`).
    *   **Vocabulary Size:** 49408 (Source: `text_encoder/config.json.txt`).
    *   **Context Length (Max Position Embeddings):** 77 (Source: `text_encoder/config.json.txt`).
*   **Tokenizer:** A `CLIPTokenizer` used to convert text prompts into tokens for the text encoder (Source: `model_index.json.txt`).
    *   **Model Max Length:** 77 tokens (Source: `tokenizer/tokenizer_config.json.txt`).
*   **Scheduler:** A `PNDMScheduler` is used to guide the diffusion (denoising) process (Source: `model_index.json.txt`).
*   **Safety Checker:** A `StableDiffusionSafetyChecker` model is included to detect not-safe-for-work (NSFW) content. It is based on a CLIP model architecture (Source: `safety_checker/config.json.txt`).

### Training details:
